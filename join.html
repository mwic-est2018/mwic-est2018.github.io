WHAT MWIC DOES:

We are an organization that supports women in the tech industry. We provide an environment where we share experiences and learning resources. We hold workshops and coding camps, and we have multiple speakers come to speak to the group.

HOW TO JOIN:

If you love computing and would like to support women in tech, then MWIC is the organization for you! If you are interested in joining or would like to learn more about us, email us at mwic@mountunion.edu.

WHEN WE MEET:

We meet every Wednesday at 4:15 pm in KHIC 057.

KEEP UP WITH US ON TWITTER:

Find us on Twitter @umuMWIC where you'll find information about meetings, women in tech spotlights, and more!

Supporting the participation of women in computing and building a strong and inclusive community.

WOMEN IN TECH SPOTLIGHT:

Carol Shaw

Carol Shaw (born 1955) is a former video game designer, notable for being one of the first female designers in the video game industry. While working at Atari, Inc. in 1978, Shaw designed the unreleased Polo game and designed 3-D Tic-Tac-Toe the same year, both for the Atari 2600.
